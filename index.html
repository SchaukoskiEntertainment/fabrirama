How to Play

Desktop

Move: Use WASD or Arrow keys

Jump: Press Spacebar

Select Item: Use the Mouse Wheel or press 1 or 2

Dig/Mine: Left-click

Place Blocks: Right-click

Mobile

Use touch controls to interact with the on-screen buttons

Move: Use the WASD or Arrow keys to move the player.

Mobile: Use the virtual joystick located in the bottom-left corner to move the player in any direction.

Camera Movement: Click the screen to lock the cursor (press Esc to unlock), then move your mouse to look around.

Mobile: Drag your finger on the screen to look around.

Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump while moving.

Mobile: Tap or hold the arrow button on the right side of the screen to jump.

After picking up the shovel, move the camera close to the dirt block until a black outline appears, then hold the left mouse button to break it.

Mobile: Approach the dirt block until a black outline appears, then hold the cross button on the right side of the screen.

You can’t jump two blocks high. Select the dirt block with the scroll wheel or 2 key, move the camera close until a black outline appears (without touching the player), then right-click to place it.

Mobile: Tap the dirt block in the toolbar, move the camera close, and tap the cross icon to place the block.

If you fall onto red blocks, you’ll lose all the items in your toolbar.

You’ve collected a pickaxe! It can only break stone. Mine stone and use it to build your way up this two-block-high area.

To complete the level, enter the blue portal.
